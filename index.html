Welcome To Food & Genes Initiative

Food and Genes Initiative is a non-profit organization aimed at creating interventions through awareness,research and technology that will improve the feeding habits and lifestyles of Africans thereby reducing non-communicable diseases and enhancing longevity and productivity in the Continent. Food & Gene Initiative is a non-governmental organisation whose specialities is in sensitizing teenagers and adult about the effect of drug abuse in the society. It was founded by Mr. Samuel Olarewaju Ogunsola in the year 2019, The organization has solved diverse issues related to drug usuage in the society and intend to propagate its awareness in regions which lack requisite knowledge on the effect of drug abuse.

Education for all

Drug and substance misuse and dependence among Nigerian youths: Codeine* Codeine is being misused by Nigerian youths at an alarming rate. Commonly known as “Lean, Codeine, or Coder” it has become a favorite for many drug abusers because it is very easy to obtain over the counter at big and small medicine stores alike. Young people often mix it with soft drinks for flavour and is usually taken with alcohol or marijuana for an extra punch. The main reason for the increased use of codeine is the euphoric effects associated with opiates. Although some people think the drug seems harmless, at high enough doses, codeine use can lead to respiratory failure, coma, and even death. This risk is especially high when it is combined with other depressants. Other effects include drowsiness, itching, nausea, vomiting, dry mouth and depression.
